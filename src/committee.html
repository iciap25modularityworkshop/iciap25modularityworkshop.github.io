Workshop Organizers

Dr. Angelo Porrello

Assistant Professor, University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, Italy

Angelo Porrello has been a member of the research group AimageLab at the Department of Engineering "Enzo Ferrari" in Modena for eight years. His primary research areas include incremental and modular training paradigms for deep neural networks and development of predictive AI systems.

Dr. Lorenzo Bonicelli

Post-Doc, University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, Italy

Lorenzo Bonicelli is a postdoctoral fellow focusing on continual learning and its applications on complex scenarios including noisy, biased, or missing annotations, rehearsal and data-free environments, multi-modal, and composition-based learning.

Pietro Buzzega

PhD Student, University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, Italy

Pietro Buzzega is a researcher at AImageLab, with work centering on incremental and modular learning paradigms for deep neural networks, with a particular emphasis on decentralized architectures.

Donato Crisostomi

PhD Student, Sapienza University of Rome, Italy

Donato Crisostomi is a researcher at Sapienza University of Rome, focusing on model merging and representational alignment. He currently leads the "Model Reuse" work package for the FIS project "NEXUS" and previously held roles at Amazon.

Dr. Simone Palazzo

Assistant Professor, University of Catania, Italy

Simone Palazzo has been pursuing research in computer vision, medical imaging, continual learning, and explainable AI. He is author of 150+ peer-reviewed scientific papers and is associate editor at ACM Transactions on Intelligent Systems and Technology.

Dr. Giovanni Bellitto

Assistant Professor, University of Catania, Italy

Giovanni Bellitto's research interests center on machine learning and artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on neuro-inspired incremental learning, federated learning and medical image analysis.
