Workshop Program

This is a tentative program for "Breaking the Monolith: 1st Workshop on Advances in Modular Deep Learning", which will be held on September 15-16, 2025 in Rome, Italy. The final program will be announced after the paper review process is completed.

Note: All times are in Central European Summer Time (CEST, UTC+2).
