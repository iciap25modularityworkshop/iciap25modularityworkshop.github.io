Explore Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, offers an unparalleled blend of history, culture, and culinary delights. While attending the workshop, take some time to explore these iconic landmarks:

Colosseum

The world's largest ancient amphitheater and one of the most iconic symbols of Rome, built nearly 2,000 years ago.

Roman Forum

A plaza surrounded by the ruins of ancient government buildings at the center of the city.

Vatican City

Home to St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums with countless masterpieces.

Trevi Fountain

The largest Baroque fountain in the city and one of the most famous fountains in the world.

Pantheon

A former Roman temple, now a church, with one of the world's most significant architectural achievements.
